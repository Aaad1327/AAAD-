About AAAD Institute:-

The Future of Education here.Classes will be start soon AAAD Institute of Technology, Roorkee has been established under Uttarakhand Govt. Act 5, 2015 in the district of Haridwar. The University campus is located in the vicinity of Holy Ganga River at Roorkee. The University campus can be easily approached by road and railway network as it is situated on Roorkee Dehradun Highway. The University is promoted by “AAAD Institute of Management & Technology Society”. The Society was established on December 23, 2004 with the objective to provide education, research and training to aspiring youth of Uttarakhand and the country in order to increase their employability.
